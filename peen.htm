Latter-day Philosophy

Daily Potential Wisdom
Day 1 - January 18, 2025

Can you have peace and desire? How can you achieve tranquility while wanting more? …… is it possible to have peace without progression? Improvement breeds calmness, knowing you are trying to better and are doing better gives relief, it triggers the exhale, allows a brief moment of pure calm to wash over you and cleanse the soul, like everything, peace is fleeting, all you can do is bathe in it as much as possible.

Day 2 - January 19, 2025

Look at yourself, not now but in the future, who you want to be, his qualities, his character, how does he act? What can he do? The ideal you, what does he have that you don't? What are you missing? Can you see it? You know the path, you always have, it is your decision whether you walk it.

Day 4 - January 21, 2025

All you need to know, breathe, think, move, the recipe for life.

Day 6 - January 22, 2025

The mind is a cloudy sky, there are days where the direction is unclear and all you can see is darkness, there are days where you can sense the storm and feel the rain as it drenches your mind and disturbs your every thought, then there are the beautiful days, the days where you can see as far as you wish with nothing but a clear direction and unwavering optimism. Unlike the weather on earth, you can control the weather in your mind, you can dictate if the sun shines through the looming gray clouds, you can decide if the rain is used to water the crops or to flood the town. You may not be able to control all the elements put in your mind but you do choose what is done with them.
